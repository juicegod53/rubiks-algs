What is PLL?

PLL (Permute Last Layer) is the final step of the CFOP method for solving the Rubik's cube. Here are algorithms for the PLL step.

Edge Cases

Ua Perm

M2' U M U2 M' U M2'

Ub Perm

M2' U' M U2' M' U' M2'

Z Perm

(M2' U' M2' U') M' (U2 M2' U2) M'

H Perm

(M2' U M2') U2 (M2' U M2')

Corner Cases

Aa Perm

x (R' U R') D2 (R U' R') D2 R2 x'

Ab Perm

x R2' D2 (R U R') D2 (R U' R) x'

E Perm

x' (R U' R' D) (R U R' D') (R U R' D) (R U' R' D') x

Diagonal Corners

Y Perm

F (R U' R' U') (R U R' F') (R U R' U') (R' F R F')

V Perm

(R' U R' U') y (R' F' R2 U') (R' U R' F) R F

Na Perm

(R U R' U) (R U R' F') (R U R' U') (R' F R2 U') R' U2 (R U' R')

Nb Perm

(R' U R U') (R' F' U' F) (R U R' F) R' F' (R U' R)

Adjacent Corners

Ra Perm

y' (L U2 L' U2) L F' (L' U' L U) L F L2' U

Rb Perm

(R' U2 R U2') R' F (R U R' U') R' F' R2 U'

Ja Perm

y' (L' U' L F) (L' U' L U) L F' L2' U L U

Jb Perm

(R U R' F') (R U R' U') R' F R2 U' R' U'

T Perm

(R U R' U') R' F R2 U' R' U' R U R' F'

F Perm

(R' U' F') (R U R' U') (R' F R2 U') (R' U' R U) (R' U R)

Edge-Corner Cycles

Ga Perm

R2 U (R' U R' U') (R U' R2) D U' (R' U R D')

Gb Perm

y' (R' U' R) U D' R2 U (R' U R U') R U' R2' D

Gc Perm

y2 L2 U' (L U' L U) (L' U L2 D') (U L U' L') D

Gd Perm

y2 (L U L') U' D L2 U' (L U' L' U) L' U L2 D'
